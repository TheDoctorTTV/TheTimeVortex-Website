Privacy Policy

At TheTimeVortex (accessible at https://thetimevortex.net), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we collect, how it’s used, and the services involved in running the site.

If you have any questions, you can reach the founder at thedoctor@thetimevortex.net.


Information Collected

TheTimeVortex does not collect or store any personal or technical information about visitors. Any data collection is handled solely by the third-party services that support the site, as described in the Third-Party Services section.


Local Storage

TheTimeVortex does not use cookies. Instead, we use your browser’s localStorage to save simple preferences, such as your chosen theme (light, dark, or system). This information never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

If you prefer, you can clear or disable localStorage in your browser settings, though some features (like theme preference) may not work as intended.


Third-Party Services

TheTimeVortex does not run ads or sell your data. The only third party involved is:

  • Cloudflare – provides DNS, CDN, and security services (like DDoS protection and performance optimization).

Cloudflare may log technical data as part of its normal operations. For more information, see their Privacy Policy.


Children’s Privacy

TheTimeVortex does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.


Online-Only Policy

This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected through thetimevortex.net and not to any offline interactions.


Consent
